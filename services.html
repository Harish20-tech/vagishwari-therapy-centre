Our Services

At Vagishwari Therapy Centre, we offer comprehensive assessment and therapy services designed to address a wide range of speech, language, communication, and hearing concerns. Each therapy program is individualized to meet the specific needs of every client.

Speech and Language Assessment

We conduct detailed evaluations to identify speech, language, voice, and fluency concerns. Our assessments help determine the nature and severity of the difficulty and guide the development of a personalized therapy plan.

Speech Therapy for Children

✔ Speech delay

✔ Language delay

✔ Articulation difficulties

✔ Stammering (fluency disorders)

✔ Autism-related communication challenges

✔ Developmental speech and language disorders

Our therapy sessions are structured, engaging, and developmentally appropriate, ensuring children receive supportive and effective intervention.

Adult Speech and Voice Therapy

We provide therapy for adults experiencing voice disorders, stammering, speech difficulties following neurological conditions, and other communication challenges. Our approach focuses on improving functional communication and restoring confidence.

Audiology Services

✔ Hearing evaluation

✔ Hearing screening

✔ Identification of hearing loss

✔ Counseling for hearing-related concerns

Early identification of hearing issues plays a crucial role in speech and language development and overall communication success.

Parent Counseling and Guidance

We believe family involvement is essential for successful outcomes. We provide guidance, home practice strategies, progress updates, and counseling to support parents and caregivers throughout the therapy journey.

Individualized Therapy Programs

Every individual receives a customized intervention plan based on assessment findings and clinical observations. Progress is regularly monitored, and therapy goals are adjusted to ensure measurable improvement.
